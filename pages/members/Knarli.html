  • KidsTube-for-Dummies-2
    • Featured question by Vinneavistafan Need help with something on KidsTube? Comment or private message me with your question and it will be answered on the next KidsTube for Dummies. 'How do you add KidsTube Members to your friends list?' Asks Workhourse Good question. Luckily I have an answer. Go on the profile of the member you wish to add to your friends list. Under their profile picture you should see 'Send me a Message.' Under that, it says 'Send me a Friend Invite.' Click that. If you wish to accept a friend invite from a member, click requests which can be found under '(Your Username)'s Stats.' On the Home Page. 'How do you upload a Profile Picture?' Asks PeachAndDash. Easy. While your logged in, under the Navigation bar, you should see 'My Account, My Videos, My Favorites, My Profile, My Friends, and so on. Click 'My Profile.' Look for where it says 'Browse...' Click that and select the image you want as your Profile Image. Remember you can delete your Profile Picture. 'What does Comments and Ratings Mean?' Asks Babygirl112. If your talking about where it says 'Comments and Ratings' under '(Your username)'s Stats.' Then it means how many comments and ratings you have on your video(s) total. 'How do you upload audio?' Asks KayliStar. You can do this by clicking 'My Account.' Under the navigation bar. Then click 'Upload New Audio.' 'What does mpg , mpeg , avi , divx , mp4 , flv , wmv , rm , mov , and moov mean?' Asks Mckster888. It's the file type of a video. Just like images! You don't need to worry to much about it. KIDSTUBE FOR DUMMIES IS SPONSORED BY KIDSTUBE NEWS! Check out their videos!
