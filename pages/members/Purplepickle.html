  • The-3-Virtual-Worlds-I-Visit
    • Okay this is my first video on KidsTube. These are some pretty awesome websites called Secret Builders, Club Penguin, and Nicktropolis.
    Secret Builders:
    A fun website for kids that is all Halloween! You can have fun, play games, earn things similar to money called Shills.
    If you join then go to your living room, it will ask where you heard of Secret Builders, say from a friend, and type in Yome009.
    Club Penguin:
    A website where you interact with other kids around the world, as a penguin! It is fun, and easy to sign up! You can buy clothes, decorate your igloo, and choose different igloo designs if you sign up for a membership, and pay a monthly fee. Members are also allowed into special places during special events in Club Penguin.
    Nicktropolis:
    A free virtual world for kids. There is newsletters, outfitters, and fun daily jobs! There are places where you can talk to the animated Spongebob, Danny Phantom, Jimmy Neutron, and Back at the Barnyard items! There are also other places to visit like the iCarly Zone, and The Naked Brothers Band Zone!
    I will make more videos on these virtual worlds! Add me!
