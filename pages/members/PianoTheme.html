  • Piano-Themes--Company-Logo
    • Welcome to PianoThemes on You Tube

    PianoThemes is Unique new approach to supplying music fans with their favorite tunes from movies and games.
    The new idea is to arrange the piece to piano, then either reinsert it or assign it to some original footage where the music came from. This is all apart from providing the sheet music for free at www.pianothemes.com.

    Now yes we do have some videos of our performer and arranger playing the keyboard to some of the tunes, sort of similar to the famous channel for Kyle556, but not nearly as many videos of course, and ours are merely experimental.

    The final stage of PianoThemes will be to have a vast amount of videos full of live piano music being played to our classic favorite movies and games.

    So Sit back, relax, and enjoy this great new experience only known as PianoThemes.
